About

Thank you for using the site!

Hi, I'm Jacob Weissman

I am a computer science major at Queens College CUNY, a huge sports fan, and a math nerd. I am the most hardcore believer in analytics: process over results, throw out the eye test, clutch is fake, etc. My hot take: when it comes to baseball, the statistics are more interesting than the sport itself. Unless it's a New York team, I can barely last through a game.

Why I Built This Website

I had always wanted to build my own model to predict games. There is nothing cooler than casually dropping "well, my model loves Baltimore today" in a conversation. About a year ago, I built a baseball model, at which point I realized that the hard work (scraping the data, gathering the matchups, and building the classes) is basically the same for all models. The last bit, which is the fun part, is where personal opinion comes into play. Why not make sports modeling available to everyone by doing the hard part myself, and providing an interface for everyone to do the fun part?
