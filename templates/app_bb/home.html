Welcome to the New Age of Analytics Accessibility
CompFolks - Built by Jacob Weissman

The field of baseball analytics is an embarrassment of riches. We have at our fingertips endless statistics for every pitch, batted ball, and player. Yet utilizing all of this information is voodoo for the common fan. Only experts have the tools to build models and predict games, and they provide no explanation of how their black boxes operate. Fans are left unaware and unfulfilled.

No longer. Behold the dawn of a new era, where building a complex model is so easy, even a scorecard-keeping geezer could do it. The common fan now has dozens of variables at their disposal. Just fill out what you want to consider in your model, provide the weights (in other words, importance), and sit back as we calculate the game odds for you.

STEP 1
SELECT YOUR CATEGORIES

Choose from a vast array of basic, advanced, pitch-tracking, and batted-ball statistics.

STEP 2
CHOOSE YOUR WEIGHTS

Fill out how heavily each statistic should be weighed when the game results are calculated.

STEP 3
CLICK RUN

The easy part! We will collect all the data, do the computations, and provide the game results.

STEP 4
VIEW AND SHARE

View the day's game odds according to your model, share with friends, and enjoy the games.
